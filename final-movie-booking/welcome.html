About Us

Proudly launched on the 12th of August 2016, in partnership with multiple distributors such as MCine and Star Cinemas in Mauritius.
We have opened our doors in commercial areas like ‘Super U’ in Central Flacq, Trianon Shopping Mall, Bagatelle Mall, Grand Baie La Croisette and Port Louis.

We have over 15 screening halls all around the island with a total seating capacity of 6000.
Our halls are equipped with cutting-edge technology, including 4K laser projectors, 12.2 surround sound speakers with overhead speakers.
Here you'll find movies from industries like Hollywood, Bollywood and even Sandalwood. We also offer some movies in multiple languages.
